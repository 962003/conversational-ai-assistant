# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I reset my password?
Go to the sign-in page, click **Forgot password**, and enter your email. You will
receive a reset link valid for 30 minutes.

## How do I change my plan?
Open **Settings → Billing → Plan** and choose a new plan. Upgrades take effect
immediately; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Can I use the assistant in multiple languages?
Yes. The assistant supports multi-language detection. Dialogflow CX agents can be
configured with multiple language codes, and Gemini responds in the user's
language.

## Is my data secure?
Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Enterprise plans
support VPC deployment and data residency controls.

## How do I talk to a human?
Just say "talk to an agent" or "I need a human". The assistant collects your name,
email, and issue, then creates a support ticket and routes you to a live agent.

## What are your support hours?
Self-service via the assistant is available 24/7. Human agents are available
Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm in your local time zone.

## Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. The Pro plan includes a **14-day free trial**, no credit card required.

## How accurate are the assistant's answers?
The assistant only answers from the approved knowledge base. If it cannot find an
answer, it says so and offers to connect you to a human, preventing made-up
responses.

# Pricing

## Subscription Plans
| Plan | Price (monthly) | Price (annual) | Best for |
|------|-----------------|----------------|----------|
| Starter | $0 | $0 | Individuals trying the product |
| Pro | $29 / user | $290 / user | Small teams |
| Business | $79 / user | $790 / user | Growing companies |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Large organizations |

## What's Included
- **Starter**: 1 seat, community support, 500 messages/month.
- **Pro**: unlimited messages, email support, analytics dashboard.
- **Business**: everything in Pro plus SSO, role-based access, priority support.
- **Enterprise**: everything in Business plus dedicated success manager, SLA,
  on-premise / VPC deployment, and custom integrations.

## Billing
- All plans are billed in **USD**.
- Annual billing saves roughly **2 months** compared to monthly.
- You can upgrade or downgrade at any time; changes are prorated.
- We accept all major credit cards and, for Enterprise, invoicing via PO.

## Discounts
- **20%** discount for registered non-profits and educational institutions.
- Volume discounts apply automatically above 50 seats.

## Taxes
Prices shown exclude applicable taxes (VAT/GST), which are calculated at checkout.

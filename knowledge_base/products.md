# Product Information

## Acme Assistant Hub
The flagship conversational AI platform. Connects Dialogflow CX, Gemini, and your
knowledge base to automate customer support.
- **SKU**: AAH-100
- **Price**: included in Pro and above
- **Key features**: intent detection, knowledge grounding, analytics, human handoff.

## Acme Voice Gateway
Adds voice (telephony) support to the assistant via SIP and the CCAI telephony
connector.
- **SKU**: AVG-200
- **Price**: $49 / month add-on
- **Key features**: speech-to-text, text-to-speech, barge-in, call transfer.

## Acme Insights
Advanced analytics and quality monitoring for conversations.
- **SKU**: AIN-300
- **Price**: $39 / month add-on
- **Key features**: sentiment trends, topic clustering, containment rate,
  CSAT tracking, exportable reports.

## Acme Connectors
Pre-built integrations for CRMs and ticketing systems (Salesforce, Zendesk,
ServiceNow, HubSpot).
- **SKU**: ACN-400
- **Price**: $19 / month per connector
- **Key features**: ticket creation, order lookup, customer profile sync.

## Compatibility
All products run on Google Cloud and integrate with Dialogflow CX agents. The
assistant supports web chat, mobile SDK, and telephony channels.

# Refund Policy

## Eligibility Window
Customers may request a refund within **30 days** of the delivery date. The item
must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the order number.

## How to Request a Refund
1. Go to **My Orders** and select the order.
2. Click **Request Refund** and choose a reason.
3. You will receive a prepaid return label by email within 24 hours.

## Refund Processing Time
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method**.
- Processing takes **5–7 business days** after we receive the returned item.
- Digital products are refunded within **48 hours** of an approved request.

## Non-Refundable Items
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software once activated
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Items marked **Final Sale**

## Partial Refunds
Partial refunds may be granted for items that are returned damaged, missing parts,
or returned more than 30 days after delivery (at our discretion).

## Contact
For refund disputes, email **refunds@acme-demo.com** or ask to speak with a human
agent and we will create a support ticket for you.

# Shipping & Order Status

## Shipping Options
| Method | Delivery time | Cost |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Standard | 5–7 business days | Free over $50, else $4.99 |
| Express | 2–3 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight | Next business day | $24.99 |

## Order Status Stages
1. **Placed** – we received your order.
2. **Processing** – payment confirmed, warehouse is preparing your package.
3. **Shipped** – your package is in transit; a tracking number is emailed to you.
4. **Out for delivery** – the carrier will deliver today.
5. **Delivered** – the package has arrived.

## Tracking Your Order
You can check status in **My Orders** or by giving the assistant your **order
number** (for example: "Where is my order 12345?"). The assistant looks up the
order and returns the current stage and estimated delivery date.

## International Shipping
- We ship to **40+ countries**.
- International delivery takes **7–21 business days** depending on customs.
- Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

## Delays
Severe weather, holidays, and customs inspections can cause delays. If your order
is more than **3 days** past its estimated delivery date, request a human agent
and we will open an investigation with the carrier.
